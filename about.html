About me

Hi I'm Chamila. All my life I was interested in modeling and working hard to thoroughly understand and solve problems. I’ve graduated from University of Missouri Kansas City with a PhD in physics with a minor in Geoscience. My research concentration is computational material science where I worked intensely envision new material and understand old materials via building computer simulation based on classical and quantum physics. After graduation I’ve worked as a post-doctoral fellow at university of Colorado boulder, Georgetown university and Illinois Institute of Technology on various materials and techniques.
Past few years I’ve learned and developed a keen sense of wonder toward data science. Thus, I’ve joined Flatiron school of computing to sharpen my knowledge of ML and AI techniques and to have hand-on experience of data science projects.
